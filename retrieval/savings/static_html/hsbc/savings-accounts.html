Cash e-ISA

Online flexible tax-free savings

INTEREST RATE

Up to 2.75% AER (2.72% tax-free) variable

MINIMUM AMOUNT TO OPEN

£1

ACCESS

Instant Access
No charge

Find out more about cash E isa


Brief highlights

  • 2.75% AER for balances over £15,000
  • 2.10% AER for balances between £9,000 and £15,000
  • 1.75% AER for balances up to £9,000

  • All interest rates are variable

Regular Saver

Save a fixed amount at a fixed rate of interest
for 12 months

INTEREST RATE

Up to 6.00% AER/gross
(selected customers only)

MINIMUM AMOUNT TO OPEN

£25

ACCESS

Fixed term of 12 months
No partial withdrawals allowed

Find out more about regular saver


Brief highlights

  • 6% AER/gross for HSBC Premier, HSBC Advance, HSBC Advance (Graduate) or HSBC Passport customers
  • 4% AER/gross for Bank Account, Bank Account Pay Monthly or Graduate Bank Account customers
  • An easy, convenient way to save for 12 months
  • A great fixed rate of interest which you will receive one year after you open your account
  • Deposit at least £25 a month. The monthly subscription limit is £250. However if you do not save £250 in any given month you can carry over any unused subscription to following months
  • No partial withdrawals allowed
  • For example: if you invest £250 a month, you will save the maximum balance of £3,000 during the 12 month term, at 6% interest, you will earn approximately £97 interest (gross)

Definitions


AER stands for Annual Equivalent Rate. This shows you what the gross rate would be if interest were paid and compounded each year.


GROSS is the interest rate before the deduction of tax applicable to interest on savings accounts.

Eligibility requirements

Please ensure you can answer YES to the following questions.

  • Are you 18 years or over?
  • Will you be paying at least £500 per month into this account?
    (If you don't make the £500 monthly payment required to maintain your account, we will either change it to a Current Account or give you notice that we will be closing your account. If you are under the age of 24, the qualification criteria will not apply.)
  • Are you happy for us to undertake a credit register search?
  • Can you provide us with identification and proof of your address and one month's bank statements from your current bank?

Once you've submitted your application, we'll send you the paperwork to sign and return to us. We may also ask for further documents to confirm your identity or address.
